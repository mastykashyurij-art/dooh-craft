The Recipe

Inside the
Cerveja do Trigo.

Two decades of refinement distilled into one wheat beer. This is the house recipe behind our flagship — built on patience, balance, and a 50% raw-wheat backbone.

The Grain Bill

50% malted wheat, 45% Pilsner malt, 5% flaked oats for body. Mashed at 65°C for 60 minutes to coax a soft, bready malt character with a dry, crisp finish.

Wheat 50% Pilsner 45% Oats 5%

Hops & Bittering

A gentle 18 IBU. Czech Saaz at 60 minutes for a clean bitter spine, then a late Hallertau Mittelfrüh addition at flame-out for soft floral and lemon-zest aromatics.

Saaz Hallertau 18 IBU

Yeast & Fermentation

Bavarian wheat yeast pitched at 18°C, free-rising to 21°C over an open 21-day primary. This is where the signature banana and clove esters bloom — never rushed, never filtered.

Weizen yeast 21 days 5.2% ABV

Conditioned cold for two weeks, then naturally carbonated. Best poured at 6°C into a tall weizen glass — one smooth motion, leaving the yeast for a final swirl. Na zdrowie.
